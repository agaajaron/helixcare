# Plan PPO-Blue-2025 — Imaging Rules

## 4.2 Lumbar MRI
Prior authorization (PA) **is required** for CPT 72148 unless:
- Red flags present (e.g., severe neurologic deficit), **or**
- Member completed **≥ 6 weeks** of conservative therapy (e.g., PT) without improvement.
